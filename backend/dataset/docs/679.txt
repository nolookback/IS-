讲座摘要：
Dietary fiber is one of the seven nutrients in human body and plays an important role in health. Dietary fiber comes from a wide variety of sources, but there is a lack of systematic research on the application mechanism of dietary fiber in food processing and its precise nutrition regulation on human health. In this report, arabinoxylan was extracted from wheat bran by-product in wheat processing, and its functional and molecular characteristics as soluble dietary fiber were studied. The relationship between arabinoxylan and phenolic compounds was elucidated, the structure-activity mechanism between arabinoxylan and starch was analyzed, and the nutritional regulation mechanism of arabinoxylan in the application process of baked food was explored. This study provides a theoretical basis for precise nutritional regulation of dietary fiber.
膳食纤维是人体七大营养素之一，对人体健康发挥着重要作用。膳食纤维来源广泛，种类繁多，但关于膳食纤维在食品加工中的应用机理及其对人体健康的精准营养调控还缺乏系统研究。本报告以小麦加工中产生的麦麸副产物为原料，从中提取阿拉伯木聚糖，研究了其作为可溶性膳食纤维的功能特性与分子特征，阐明了其与酚类物质复合的作用关系，分析了其与淀粉互作之间的构效机制，并探索了其在焙烤食品应用过程中的营养调控机理。本研究为膳食纤维的精准营养调控提供了理论依据。

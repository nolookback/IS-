报告摘要：
This lecture has
particular relevance to research students, post-docs, and academic staff in
science and engineering. The starting point of this lecture is that one has a
set good results from recent research. This lecture will outline how to
construct a paper – how to choose a title, how to write an abstract, how to
write conclusions, how to presents results, when to start writing the paper,
and many other things (e.g., references, which journal).

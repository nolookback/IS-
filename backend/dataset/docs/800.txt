报告摘要
：
With the advance of high throughput
sequencing, multi-dimensional data are generated. Detecting
dependence/correlation between these datasets is becoming one of most important
issues in multi-dimensional data integration and co-expression network construction.
In this talk, we presented several new indexes for testing both linear and
nonlinear correlation between two matrices. Further, these indexes were applied
to infer gene-isoform co-expression networks by using exon-level RNA-Seq data
and successfully predict the distinct biological functions of gene isoforms.
This is a joint work with Dr. Dingjie Wang, Dr. Zhihao Yao and Prof. KinFai Au.

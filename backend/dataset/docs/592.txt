讲座主要内容：
Using a comprehensive sample of over 10,000 bank loan announcements identified from 8 K filings, we show that bank loan financing continues to convey valuable private information as the regulatory environment changes over time. The insignificant wealth effect of bank loan announcements documented in several early studies is potentially driven by small sample size or less important loans announced in the news. New evidence suggests that the positive announcement effect is stronger for deals with higher materiality, more favorable pricing, larger lead bank shares, and higher syndicate concentration, when lenders have higher credit quality, and when credit market conditions are worse.

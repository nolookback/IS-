讲座主要内容：
Using
News Corp.
’
s acquisition of Dow Jones
& Co. in 2007 as a natural experiment, we examine the role of the media in
hedge fund activism. We find that firms with more negative media sentiment are
more likely to be targeted by hedge funds. More importantly, this relationship
is strengthened after the acquisition for those hedge funds that invest in News
Corp. Negative media sentiment helps activists to achieve their campaign
objectives and leads to higher long-term stock returns. Finally, we provide
suggestive evidence on the mechanism through which ownership of the media leads
to biased reports.

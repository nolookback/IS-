土木与交通工程学院　2021/10/12 11:07:00
College of Civil and Transportation Engineering
Series of Lectures on Green Energy System Research
——
Series of Academic Lectures of Prof. Katsunori Nagano, Hokkaido University, Japan
报告人
：长野克则，日本北海道大学教授
Reporter
: Katsunori Nagano,Professor
of Hokkaido University
报告背景：
依托国家重点研发计划中日国际合作项目《基于石墨烯
/
碳纤维改性水泥基材料和绿色能源综合应用技术的环境自适应型道路体系研究》，邀请日本北海道大学长野克则教授开展系列学术讲座。此次学术系列报告将着重介绍报告人对绿色能源系统的研究，主要涉及到提高能源利用效率、降低系统寿命周期成本的创新技术、以及绿色能源循环系统的温度监控预测数值模型。
Report background:
Relying on the
national key research and development plan Sino-Japanese international
cooperation project "Research on Environmentally Adaptive Road System
Based on Graphene/Carbon Fiber Modified Cementitous Materials and Green Energy
Comprehensive Application Technology", Pro. Katsunori Nagano, Hokkaido
University, Japan was invited to carry out the series academic lectures. This
academic report series will focus on the reporter's research on the green
energy system, mainly referred to innovative technologies that improve energy
efficiency and reduce system life cycle costs, and the temperature monitoring
and prediction numerical model of the green energy cycle system.

Resume of the
reporter:
Katsunori Nagano,
Professor of Faculty of Engineering,
Division of Environmental Engineering, Hokkaido University, Japan, Visiting
Professor of Harbin Institute of Technology. He currently severs as assistant
president of Hokkaido University, Chair of Research Group of Heat Pump System
and Ground Thermal Energy System in Heat Pump & Thermal Energy Storage
Center Technology of Japan (HPTC), Chairman of New Energy Introduction advisory
board of Date-City. Katsunori Nagano has been engaged in the technical design
and  simulated modeling of green energy
system. In the past 5 years, he has presided over 5 scientific foundations of
the Japan Society for the Promotion of Science (JSPS), applied for 33
industrial property rights, published 165 papers, including more than 60 SCI
papers, and won 17 scientific and technological awards. He is an
internationally renowned expert in solar thermal utilization, heat storage, and
ground source heat pump technology applications.

讲座摘要：
Alternating current (AC) electrolysis also can tune additional parameters such as the waveform, frequency, duty ratio and so on, which could lead to some advances in reaction development. Herein we present two AC electrolysis strategies aiming at different organic synthesis scenarios. One is AC-promoted metal-catalyzed electrolysis.

The long-standing challenge of noble metal catalyst reduction on the cathode during undivided electrolysis has been successfully addressed by utilized asymmetric-waveform alternating current electrolysis.

讲座主要内容：
Using
data from a lending platform that switched from a human-based to a machine
learning-based system, we find that FinTech enables lenders to price
discriminate as the machine learning algorithms allow the platform to better
decipher borrower preferences. Specifically, the platform assigned higher
interest rates and better credit ratings to less price-sensitive female
borrowers after the switch. The results are not driven by changes in borrower
credit risk or lender preferences. Instead, the behavior is consistent with the
platform trying to maximize origination and servicing fees using price
discrimination.

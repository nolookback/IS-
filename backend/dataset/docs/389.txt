报告摘要：
The "Human x Car x Home" smart ecosystem is a rapidly growing
field that aims to seamlessly integrate technology into our daily lives.
Antenna technologies play a crucial role in this ecosystem, enabling
communication between devices and providing connectivity to the internet.
However, there are several challenges that must be addressed in order to fully
realize the potential of this ecosystem, such as satellite direct to cell, 5.5G
Communication, Digital wireless car key. These challenges include the need for
antennas that are small, low-cost, and capable of operating in a wide range of
frequencies. Additionally, antennas must be able to operate in complex
environments, such as inside buildings and vehicles, where signal interference
and multipath propagation can be significant. This report will explore the
current state of antenna technologies for the "Human x Car x Home"
smart ecosystem, as well as the challenges that must be overcome to enable
widespread adoption of these technologies.

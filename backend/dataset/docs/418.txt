【学术讲座】身心调节技术：迈向大脑健康和人类幸福之路
心理学院　2024/4/24 11:01:00
讲座名称：
身心调节技术：迈向大脑健康和人类幸福之路
讲座时间：
2024年4月26日 星期五 下午15:00
讲座地点：
深圳大学沧海校区致理楼C座L3-1201
主讲人：
魏高峡 研究员
讲座摘要：
Tai Chi Chuan is a typical mind-body integrative technique with multiple components, which includes aerobic exercise, mindfulness practice and attention training. Accumulative evidences have shown its benefits on enhancing cognitive function and alleviating negative mood as well as treating psychiatric disorders as complementary medicine. However, the neural mechanism underlying these behavioral improvement remains largely unknown. In this talk, I will present recent studies from our lab to demonstrate the key brain regions or brain networks associated with the mental health. Notably, I will introduce the recent findings on the effect of Tai Chi Chuan practice on interoception and some clinical studies involved with abnormal interoception. A new neural circuit including prefrontal cortex and insula cortex is proposed and emphasized. Interoception might be a crucial mechanism to advance better understanding of mental health induced by mind-body practice. These explorations might be of implication for the future investigations in exercise psychology.
个人简介：
魏高峡，中国科学院心理研究所研究员，哈佛医学院麻省总医院精神科访问科学家。聚焦于“人类运动行为与大脑健康”研究方向，发表SCI/SSCI期刊论文80余篇，其中部分发表在Annals of Internal Medicine、JAMA Network Open、Human Brain Mapping 等期刊上，获2项国家自然科学基金、北京市科技计划奖和教育部科学技术进步奖。目前担任中国体育科学学会运动心理学分会委员、中国心理学会体育运动专业委员会委员、中国心理学会行为与健康心理学专业委员会委员等，同时担任多个英文期刊编委和副编委等。

报告摘要：
Cooperative perception, which has a broader perception field than
single-vehicle perception, has played an increasingly important role in
autonomous driving to conduct 3D object detection. Through vehicle-to-vehicle
(V2V) communication technology, various connected automated vehicles (CAVs) can
share their sensory information (LiDAR point clouds) for cooperative
perception. In this talk, we introduce a novel cooperative perception semantic
communication scheme with intermediate fusion, based on the importance map. We
further extend the architecture to the challenging time-varying multipath
fading channel. We also investigate an error detection method at the semantic
level that is integrated within our semantic communication framework alongside
hybrid automatic repeated request (HARQ), to enhance transmission reliability. We
demonstrate that our proposed model, while trained over one specific channel, learns
robust coded representations of semantic information that remains resilient to
various channel models, showcasing its generality and robustness.

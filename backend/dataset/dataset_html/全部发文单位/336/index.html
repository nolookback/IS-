【学术讲座】Research and Development of Deep Learning-based Methods for Unsafe Driving Behavior Detection
电子与信息工程学院　2024/6/19 16:13:00
  （钟沛基 2022280095）


 

嘉 宾 ：Dr. Y.M. Tang, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University

邀请人：张勇 教授

时 间：6月20日14：30

地 点：深圳大学汇星楼1502

 

摘要：

With the development of transportation and the increase in the number of vehicles, more and more people begin to pay attention to the important topic of traffic safety, and it is believed that the analysis of unsafe driving behavior can effectively reduce the occurrence of traffic accidents and ensure traffic safety, so it is of great significance to study and analyze the driving behavior of drivers. This presentation and workshop will focus on deep learning-based detection of unsafe driving behavior with multiple data sources. After a brief introduction of the topic and research background, the latest progress of related research will be discussed in depth. This presentation and workshop will also focus on the research methodology used in the study, model selection, and especially the presentation of the effect of processing and categorizing multi-modal driving distraction data for different models. In addition, this presentation will also introduce the current work progress of the topic and the problems encountered during the research process. In addition, various factors will be synthesized and analyzed to propose possible future directions for the topic, demonstrating to the audience the long-term significance of the topic and the sustainability of the research.

嘉宾个人简介：

Dr. YM Tang received his B.Eng. and M.Phil. from CUHK. He was awarded a Ph.D. degree from the same university. Currently, he is a Senior Teaching Fellow in the Department of Industrial and Systems Engineering, PolyU. Dr. Tang is the Lab-in-charge of Integrated Product Design (IPD) in the department. His research interests include virtual reality (VR)/ mixed reality (MR), artificial intelligence (AI), blockchain technology, digital twin, sustainable technology in industry 4.0 and healthcare applications. Dr. Tang has published over 10 books/ book chapters and over 60 renowned journal articles.

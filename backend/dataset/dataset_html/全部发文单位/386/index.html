【学术讲座】Research on personalized recommendation algorithm based on meta-learning

  （钟沛基 2022280095）


 

嘉 宾 ：Dr. Y.M. Tang，香港理工大学

邀请人：张勇 教授

时间：2024年5月23日15:00

地点：深圳大学汇星楼1502

 

摘要：

As an important method to alleviate the problem of information overload in the Internet era, recommendation algorithm can automatically screen the content of interest for users from the huge amount of information on the Internet. Classic recommendation algorithms typically learn from a user's historical clicks to model their personalized interest preferences and predict how likely they are to be interested in candidate content. However, in the real scenario, new users will enter the system every day, and the model can only obtain a small amount of initial clicks for each new user, so it cannot produce accurate and reasonable recommendation results, which is also known as the user cold start problem. At the same time, with the development of meta-learning related research, it has been widely used in many scenes with insufficient samples, and has achieved remarkable results. Our research focuses on the application of meta-learning method to alleviate the user cold start problem in the recommendation system, so as to improve the performance of the recommendation system. 嘉宾个人简介：

Dr. YM Tang received his B.Eng. and M.Phil. from CUHK. He was awarded a Ph.D. degree from the same university. His research interests include virtual reality (VR)/ mixed reality (MR), artificial intelligence (AI), blockchain technology, digital twin, sustainable technology in industry 4.0 and healthcare applications.

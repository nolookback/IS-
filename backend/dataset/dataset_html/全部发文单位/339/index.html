报告摘要：

随着全球气温的升高和制冷需求的激增，被动辐射致冷技术成为了近年来世界范围内的研究热点。本次报告将系统介绍辐射致冷技术的原理、辐射致冷材料的结构以及辐射致冷技术的应用。此外，冯驰教授还将分享其团队近年来在辐射致冷方面取得的研究进展，并展望该技术的未来研究方向。

As global temperatures rise and the demand for cooling soars, passive radiative cooling technology has emerged as a hot research topic worldwide in recent years. This presentation will systematically introduce the principles of radiative cooling technology, the structure of radiative cooling materials, and the applications of radiative cooling technology. In addition, Professor Feng Chi will share the research progress his team has made in the field of radiative cooling in recent years and provide an outlook on future research directions for this technology.

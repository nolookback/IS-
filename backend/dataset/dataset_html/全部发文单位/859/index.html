讲座题目：Employment protection and debt concentration: International evidence

讲座时间：2022年6月17日（周五）14:00

讲座地点：腾讯会议（会议号：140-923-854）

主讲嘉宾：郑晓岚副教授  宁波诺丁汉大学

主讲嘉宾简介：

　　郑晓岚博士在美国南卡罗莱纳大学获得博士学位，CFA持证人。郑晓岚博士的研究兴趣主要集中在资本结构、债务期限、股利政策、资本成本、商业信用、公司治理、银行业、国际金融和腐败、文化对公司财务决策的影响上。她的文章被收录于Journal of Financial and Quantitative Analysis, Review of Finance, Journal of International Business Studies, Journal of Corporate Finance, Journal of Banking and Finance, and Journal of Business Ethics等国际一流水平期刊中。

讲座主要内容：

We examine the effects of variations in employment protections on corporate debt structure concentration. We rely on a framework of optimal debt concentration that poses a trade-off between the benefits of reducing financial distress costs and the resulting credit constraints (credit constraint concerns) or costs of inducing strategic default (strategic concerns). We test the divergent predictions from two concerns using an international sample of 118,131 firm-year observations from 40 countries over the 2001-2018 period. We find that when employment protection is stronger firms use fewer debt types suggesting credit constraint concerns outweigh strategic concerns in choosing debt structure concentration. Our findings are robust to sensitivity tests using alternative measurements for debt concentration and employment protection legislation, employment protection reform, country-level regressions, additional omitted variables, and different subsamples. The positive relationship between employment protection and debt concentration is more pronounced in the most strongly influenced firms, including those with greater labor intensity and turnover, those in countries with greater union density, and those with better law and order. Additional analysis shows stronger effects of employment protection on debt concentration in financially constrained firms, firms with greater information asymmetry, and firms in the countries where larger benefits from efficient coordination of concentrated debt derives.

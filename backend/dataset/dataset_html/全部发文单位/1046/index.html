主讲嘉宾简介：

　　马勇，湖南大学金融与统计学院教授，博士生导师。湖湘青年英才支持计划和湖南省121创新人才培养工程对象。在Journal of Futures Markets, Quantitative Finance，International Review of Finance和管理科学学报、管理工程学报等国内外重要期刊发表学术论文20余篇。承担国家自然科学基金项目2项和其他省部级项目5项。

讲座主要内容：

We investigate the effect of institutional investor attention and the public's uncertainty about asset value on the financial market by studying a strategic trading problem, where the strategic trader is subject to attention constraint and the asset value follows a mean-reverting process. We show that the limited institutional investor attention endogenously generates a private signal for the strategic trader, precluding a fully revealed price. In contrast, the transparency of the asset value improves the market efficiency. Furthermore, the trading aggressiveness, expected payoff, and informativeness of price increase with institutional investor attention, while market liquidity decreases with this attention. On the other hand, the effect of the public's uncertainty about asset value is uncertain and depends on the remainder of the strategic trader's information advantage. This feature explains different empirical findings pertaining to the relationship between public news and market liquidity.

讲座名称：A neurocomputational probe into the development of social communicative ability

讲座时间：2024年12月6日下午3点

讲座地点：深圳大学致理楼L3-1201

主讲人：朱露莎教授


讲座简介：

Humans possess a remarkable ability to communicate with others, inferring what is meant from what is and is not being said. Despite recent progress in understanding the neural bases of this important class of social function, little is known about when and how humans acquire this ability, whether basic neurocognitive functions such as executive control and social reasoning interact with one another in support of communicative development, and how individual differences in autism spectrum traits influence communicative reasoning across development?  In this talk, I will show a series of studies that use model-based fMRI with cross-sectional and longitudinal data to elucidate the neurobehavioral changes in communicative reasoning during the transition in and out of adolescence––a critical period for cognitive and social development.


主讲人简介：

朱露莎, 北京大学博雅特聘教授, 北京大学心理与认知科学学院，IDG麦戈文脑科学研究所、北大-清华生命科学联合中心研究员, 国家杰出青年科学基金、海外高层次青年人才计划获得者。从事决策神经科学方向研究，采用交叉学科研究方法，从计算、认知算法和人脑执行过程的角度探寻人类决策行为底层的动机、推理和策略。

讲座名称： From brain to mind - Spatiotemporal Neuroscience

讲座时间：2023年12月19日 星期二下午 15：00

讲座地点：深圳大学沧海校区致理楼C座L3-1201

主讲人：Georg Northoff教授

讲座摘要：

Neuroscience made major progress in understanding the neural correlates of sensory, motor, cognitive and affective functions. Despite these advances, the relationship of brain and mind remains yet unclear, though. Specifically, the exact mechanisms how neural activity transforms into mental activity as during consciousness, self and their changes in mental disorders, are currently not known. Addressing this gap in our knowledge, I present a novel approach, namely Spatiotemporal Neuroscience, that allows integrating neural and mental features through their shared spatial topographic and temporal dynamic features as their “common currency” (Northoff et al. 2020). Such spatiotemporal approach is illustrated by various lines of data and modelling on consciousness, self, and a mental disorder, e.g., depression. I finally outline some core features of Spatiotemporal Neuroscience concluding that it provides the promise of an integrated brain mind model.

个人简介：

Georg Northoff教授，加拿大皇家学会院士。渥太华大学心灵、脑影像与神经伦理学研究所主任，是全球知名的哲学家、神经科学家、精神病学家。他的研究专注于大脑和心灵的关系，揭示自我、意识等心理特征的构建机制。他提出了“时空神经科学”和精神病理学，解答大脑与心灵如何共享时间和空间的问题。他也是整合哲学、精神病学和神经科学的重要人物之一，发展了非还原性神经哲学。他发表了400多篇期刊文章和20本著作，其中多部已被翻译成多国语言，如《Neuro-philosophy and the Healthy Mind》（2016年，Norton出版社）和《Neurowaves》（2023年，McGill出版社）。

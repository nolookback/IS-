讲座名称：The neural representation of abstraction in working memory

讲座时间：2025年1月7日上午10：00

讲座地点：深圳大学沧海校区致理楼C座L3-1336

主讲人：俞青

讲座简介：

Working memory, the ability to temporarily maintain and manipulate information to serve flexible behavioral goals, is central to human intelligence and fundamental to the field of cognitive neuroscience. While it has been proposed that (long-term) memories are not mere reflections of past perceptual experiences but rather re-representations of the past, whether and how abstraction occurs in working memory remains largely unexplored. In this talk, I will present our recent work investigating the neural mechanisms underlying abstraction processes in human working memory. Using functional magnetic resonance imaging (fMRI) and electroencephalography (EEG) in combination with behavioral paradigms requiring manipulation of working memory contents, I will demonstrate the neural coding principles for abstract task information and for structural generalization in working memory across distributed cortical regions, and discuss how abstraction in working memory contributes to the execution of higher cognitive functions.

个人简介：

俞青博士目前担任中国科学院脑科学与智能技术卓越创新中心（神经科学研究所）研究员、博士生导师、视觉认知与记忆研究组组长。于北京大学获得心理学与数学学士双学位，美国达特茅斯学院获得认知神经科学博士学位，美国威斯康星大学麦迪逊分校先后从事博士后与研究科学家的研究工作。俞青博士的工作结合多模态脑成像技术（功能性核磁共振、脑磁图、头皮脑电、颅内脑电）、行为测量、数学模型等多学科手段，系统地探究工作记忆、想象、抽象认知等过程的认知神经机制。研究成果以第一或通讯作者发表于PLOS Biology (2024, 2020a, 2020b)，Current Biology (2019)，Cell Reports (2023)，eLife (2024)，Journal of Neuroscience (2018, 2023)，Cerebral Cortex (2019, 2024)，NeuroImage (2017)等期刊。担任eLife的评审编辑和Journal of Cognitive Neuroscience的顾问编辑。

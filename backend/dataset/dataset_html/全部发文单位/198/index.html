讲座题目：中文阅读中的语音加工

主讲人：潘敬儿

报告人简介：

潘敬儿博士毕业于北京师范大学认知神经科学与学习国家重点实验室，在德国波茨坦大学完成博士后训练。现为香港教育大学副教授。潘博士的研究主要集中在以下两个方面：（1）阅读发展和障碍的早期预测；（2）不同阅读能力的个体在阅读和类阅读任务中的眼动。潘博士担任Scientific Studies of Reading杂志的編委。在Cognition，Behavior Research Methods, Psychonimic Bulletin & Review, Journal of Experimental Psychology: Learning, Memory, & Cognition, Journal of Educational Psychology, Journal of Child Psychology & Psychiatry等期刊上发表文章。

讲座摘要：

Writing systems vary in how readers access word meanings from their written forms. Phonology plays a crucial role in visual word recognition in alphabetic languages. In contrast, in logographic scripts such as Chinese, readers can access word meanings before activating their phonological representations. This has been shown not only in individual word recognition but also in word processing during reading sentences. When reading sentences, individuals process the word they are currently focused on, as well as words nearby in their peripheral vision. Researchers are interested in the time course of processing different types of linguistic properties of words in both fovea and parafovea. In this presentation, I will introduce several eye-tracking studies examining how different phonological units of Chinese characters are processed during Chinese sentence reading. Our results challenge the idea that phonology universally mediates lexical access.

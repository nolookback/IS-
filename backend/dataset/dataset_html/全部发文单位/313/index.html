摘要：

Under the background of electric power safety production, the frequent occurrence of electric power accidents not only causes huge loss of life and property to the employees of electric power production, but also poses a serious threat to the stable operation of electric power system. In order to effectively prevent the occurrence of accidents, it is very important to analyze the causes of accidents deeply. However, due to the lack of structured processing of traditional accident data, it is difficult to support the in-depth cause analysis and the formulation of prevention strategies. In view of this, this presentation and workshop will aim to how to build a knowledge graph based on power accident events, which aims to transform unstructured accident event data into structured knowledge that can be calculated and queried, and assist accident prevention through intelligent analysis. Therefore, this presentation will introduce the relevant methods of deep learning to build the power safety knowledge graph, conducts in-depth research and analysis on the named entity recognition model and relationship extraction model involved in the construction of the power safety knowledge graph, and correlates the knowledge of related fields to build the power safety knowledge graph.

讲座名称：Neural indicators of individual differences in pain

讲座时间：2024年12月25号上午10点

讲座地点：深圳大学沧海校区致理楼C座L3-1336

主讲人：张立波博士

主讲人简介：

张立波，Italian Institute of Technology博士后，博士毕业于中国科学院心理研究所。致力于利用脑电、功能磁共振等技术挖掘疼痛的神经指标。在Cell Reports Medicine、NeuroImage等期刊发表论文20多篇。曾获中国科学院院长特别奖、北京市优秀毕业生等荣誉。

讲座简介：

Pain perception varies widely across individuals. The same pain stimulus may be intolerably painful for someone, yet barely perceivable by another. What neural activity encodes such dramatic individual differences in pain perception? In this talk, I classify individual differences in pain into two broad categories: pain sensitivity and pain discriminability, corresponding to the absolute and difference thresholds in psychophysics. I will then present two recent studies that developed replicable and generalizable neural indicators of these pain individual differences using large scale datasets. Altogether, these studies advance the understanding of pain mechanisms and facilitating individualized pain treatment.

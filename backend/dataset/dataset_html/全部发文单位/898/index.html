讲座题目：Social Transmission Bias: Evidence from an Online Investor Platform

讲座时间：2022年4月22日9:00-10:30

讲座地点：腾讯会议（会议号：758-592-205）

主讲嘉宾：王宝链  佛罗里达大学

主讲嘉宾简介：

　　王宝链，现为佛罗里达商学院金融系助理教授，他的研究兴趣在资产定价、行为金融、金融科技和中国经济等领域。他的研究成果发表在Journal of Finance, Journal of Financial Economics, Review of  Financial Studies, Management Science, Review of Finance, Critical Finance Review, and Strategic Management Journal等期刊上。王宝链在清华大学获得学士和硕士学位，以及在香港科技大学获得博士学位。

讲座主要内容：

Using data from a Twitter-like investor social trading platform, we document evidence consistent with the self-enhancing transmission bias. Investors are more likely to broadcast their better-performing stocks. Their followers are more likely to buy the broadcasted stocks than the non-broadcasted stocks, and the strength of the follow-up purchase is related to the perceived credibility of the broadcasts. Both the strength of the selected broadcasting and the strength of the follow-up purchase vary with stock characteristics in a way that facilitates the spread of high-variance, high-skewness, and extrapolative strategies. We also document that the social network features influential, disproportionately important nodes.

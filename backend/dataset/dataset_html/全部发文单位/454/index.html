报告人简介：

Won-Jin Kwak received his Ph.D. degree in Energy Engineering from Hanyang University. He was a Postdoctorate Research Associate in the Energy and Environment Directorate at Pacific Northwest National Laboratory and currently is an Assistant Professor in the School of Energy and Chemical Engineering at UNIST. His current research interest focuses on the development of next generation batteries and novel battery recycling strategies.

报告题目：Developing high-throughput (bio)analytical workflows to explore plant metabolism and their effects on human health

主讲嘉宾：Victor Castro-Alves 研究员

报告主持：刘宏涛  教授

报告时间：2024年10月17日（星期四）上午9:30

报告地点：深圳大学丽湖校区守慧楼A6-501

主讲嘉宾简介：

Victor Castro-Alves is a researcher at Örebro University, specializing in food science and metabolomics. He holds a PhD in Food Science from the University of São Paulo and joined the Man-Technology-Environment Research Centre (MTM), Örebro University, Sweden, in 2019. He currently coordinates a Starting Grant from the Swedish Research Council, and his research focuses on developing and applying foodomics-based (bio)analytical workflows to explore the relationship between plant metabolism and human health.

【学术讲座】Human escaping decisions: Mechanistic insights from intracranial recordings
心理学院　2024/1/2 10:29:00
  （钟沛基 2022280095）

                                                    　

讲座名称：Human escaping decisions: Mechanistic insights from intracranial recordings

讲座时间：2024年01月05日 星期五 上午10：00

讲座地点：深圳大学沧海校区致理楼C座L3-1336

主讲人：伍海燕 助理教授

讲座摘要：

Escape is a crucial aspect of defensive behavior, and it relies on two distinct circuits: the reactive fear circuity and the cognitive fear circuity. However, the precise contributions of these circuits to specific stages of human escaping remain poorly understood. In this study, we conducted intracranial electroencephalography (iEEG) recordings from epilepsy patients while they performed a modified flight initiation distance task.  We found that the high gamma activity of the brain regions in fear circuity encoded the threatening information. On the other hand, the reactive fear circuits exhibited higher high gamma activity during the actual escaping stage, particularly in response to rapid attacks. Furthermore, we observed that the amygdala suppresses the cognitive fear circuit under rapid attacks.  These novel insights highlight the collaborative involvement of both reactive and cognitive fear circuits in orchestrating the entire human escaping process.  By elucidating the interplay between these circuits, our study provides valuable contributions to our understanding of the neural mechanisms underlying defensive behavior in humans.

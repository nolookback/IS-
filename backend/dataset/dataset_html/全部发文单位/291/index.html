【学术讲座】Advancements in Multimodal Driver Distraction Detection Systems: A Systematic Review and Deployment Analysis

嘉 宾 ：Dr. Y.M. Tang, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University

邀请人：张勇 教授

地 点 ：深圳大学汇星楼1502

 

摘要：

By comprehensively reviewing the development of multimodal driver distraction detection systems and providing an in-depth analysis of key components and deployment considerations, we fill a gap in recent review articles on multimodal approaches and deployment analysis. By searching mainstream databases for 877 scientific papers from the last 10 years, 99 papers were screened for systematic summarization using inclusion and exclusion criteria. We focus on applications of visual, physiological, and vehicular sensors, including their strengths and limitations in capturing driver behavior, and propose new sensor classification methods based on commonly used sensor combinations. In addition, we delve into various multimodal data sources, such as visual, vehicular, and physiological signals, to examine the validity of multimodal data, as well as summarize the application of different feature fusion techniques and classification and recognition algorithms for multimodal distracted driving detection. We also analyze the problems that may be encountered when deploying a multimodal distracted driving detection system, considering practical factors such as cost, computational resources, and real-time processing capabilities. We also summarized that driver distraction is associated with various environmental factors such as weather, living area, and traffic section. These works provide important references and guidance for further improving the driver distraction detection system, which is expected to have a positive impact on improving driving safety and comfort.

嘉宾个人简介：

Dr. YM Tang received his B.Eng. and M.Phil. from CUHK. He was awarded a Ph.D. degree from the same university. Currently, he is a Senior Teaching Fellow in the Department of Industrial and Systems Engineering, PolyU. Dr. Tang is the Lab-in-charge of Integrated Product Design (IPD) in the department. His research interests include virtual reality (VR)/ mixed reality (MR), artificial intelligence (AI), blockchain technology, digital twin, sustainable technology in industry 4.0 and healthcare applications. Dr. Tang has published over 10 books/ book chapters and over 60 renowned journal articles.

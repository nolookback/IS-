讲座题目：The Role of the Media in Hedge Fund Activism: Evidence from a Natural Experiment

讲座时间：2021年11月9日（周二）11:00

讲座地点：明德楼402

主讲嘉宾：王新杰  南方科技大学金融系

主讲嘉宾简介：

　　王新杰助理教授2016年毕业于美国罗格斯大学(Rutgers University), 获金融学博士。此前分别在北京大学和罗格斯大学获得物理学学士和博士学位，在摩根大通银行(纽约)担任过资深量化分析师。他的主要研究方向为场外交易市场，市场微观结构，金融市场有效性，交易策略，信用风险，公司金融，新闻媒体和金融监管等。他的主要研究成果发表在Journal of Financial Economics, Journal of Financial Markets, Journal of Banking and Finance, Financial Management等国际期刊上。

讲座主要内容：

Using News Corp.’s acquisition of Dow Jones & Co. in 2007 as a natural experiment, we examine the role of the media in hedge fund activism. We find that firms with more negative media sentiment are more likely to be targeted by hedge funds. More importantly, this relationship is strengthened after the acquisition for those hedge funds that invest in News Corp. Negative media sentiment helps activists to achieve their campaign objectives and leads to higher long-term stock returns. Finally, we provide suggestive evidence on the mechanism through which ownership of the media leads to biased reports.

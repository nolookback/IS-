摘要：

The utilization of General Artificial Intelligence (GAI) as a personal intelligent tutor has the potential to revolutionize teaching and learning. By leveraging GAI's vast knowledge and computational power, learners can benefit from personalized guidance, adaptability, and continuous feedback throughout their educational journeys. This seminar explores the development of employing GAI in the role of an intelligent virtual tutor.

Personalized learning has long been recognized as an effective approach to cater to the diverse needs and preferences of learners. GAI's ability to analyze extensive amounts of data and adapt to individual learning styles offers unprecedented opportunities for customized instruction. By employing sophisticated algorithms and machine learning techniques, GAI can assess learners' strengths, weaknesses, and learning patterns, enabling the development of tailored curricula and adaptive learning pathways.

The integration of GAI as a personal intelligent tutor goes beyond the mere delivery of content. It encompasses a range of interactive features that enhance the learning experience. GAI can engage learners through conversational interfaces, simulating human-like interactions and fostering a supportive learning environment. Moreover, GAI's continuous monitoring and assessment capabilities enable real-time feedback, swiftly identifying misconceptions and providing targeted interventions to address learning gaps.

Series of Lectures on Green Energy System Research——Series of Academic Lectures of Prof. Katsunori Nagano, Hokkaido University, Japan

报告人：长野克则，日本北海道大学教授

Reporter: Katsunori Nagano,Professor of Hokkaido University

报告背景：依托国家重点研发计划中日国际合作项目《基于石墨烯/碳纤维改性水泥基材料和绿色能源综合应用技术的环境自适应型道路体系研究》，邀请日本北海道大学长野克则教授开展系列学术讲座。此次学术系列报告将着重介绍报告人对绿色能源系统的研究，主要涉及到提高能源利用效率、降低系统寿命周期成本的创新技术、以及绿色能源循环系统的温度监控预测数值模型。

Report background: Relying on the national key research and development plan Sino-Japanese international cooperation project "Research on Environmentally Adaptive Road System Based on Graphene/Carbon Fiber Modified Cementitous Materials and Green Energy Comprehensive Application Technology", Pro. Katsunori Nagano, Hokkaido University, Japan was invited to carry out the series academic lectures. This academic report series will focus on the reporter's research on the green energy system, mainly referred to innovative technologies that improve energy efficiency and reduce system life cycle costs, and the temperature monitoring and prediction numerical model of the green energy cycle system.

报告人简历：长野克则，日本北海道大学工学院环境工程系教授，哈尔滨工业大学客座教授。现担任北海道大学校长助理、日本热泵与热能储存中心技术(HPTC)主任、新能源引进咨询委员会主席等职务。长野克则长期从事绿色能源系统的技术设计与建模仿真工作，近5年主持5项日本学术振兴会（JSPS）科学基金，申请33项专利，发表论文165篇，其中SCI论文60余篇，并获各项科技奖励17项，是一位太阳能热利用、蓄热、地源热泵技术应用方面的国际知名专家。

Resume of the reporter: Katsunori Nagano, Professor of Faculty of Engineering, Division of Environmental Engineering, Hokkaido University, Japan, Visiting Professor of Harbin Institute of Technology. He currently severs as assistant president of Hokkaido University, Chair of Research Group of Heat Pump System and Ground Thermal Energy System in Heat Pump & Thermal Energy Storage Center Technology of Japan (HPTC), Chairman of New Energy Introduction advisory board of Date-City. Katsunori Nagano has been engaged in the technical design and  simulated modeling of green energy system. In the past 5 years, he has presided over 5 scientific foundations of the Japan Society for the Promotion of Science (JSPS), applied for 33 industrial property rights, published 165 papers, including more than 60 SCI papers, and won 17 scientific and technological awards. He is an internationally renowned expert in solar thermal utilization, heat storage, and ground source heat pump technology applications.

【学术讲座】Mode of action of bacterial type III effectors and plant immunity orchestrated by salicylic acid

报告主题: Mode of action of bacterial type III effectors and plant immunity orchestrated by salicylic acid

报告主持：刘宏涛  教授

主 讲 嘉宾：傅正擎 教授（美国南卡罗来纳大学）

报告时间：2025年1月8日（星期三）上午9：30

报告地点：深圳大学丽湖校区守慧楼A6-501

Abstract:

Plant diseases result in an annual economic loss exceeding $220 billion worldwide. Gram-negative plant bacterial pathogens reply on type III secretion system and type III effectors to cause disease. Various type III effectors, like HopU1 and AvrPtoB, function as enzymes to undermine plant innate immunity. Through research using the model plant Arabidopsis thaliana, scientists have identified key players molecular interactions between plants and pathogens.  NPR1, NPR3, and NPR4 have been identified as receptors for the plant defense hormone salicylic acid. Additionally, EDS1 serves as a central hub in pathogen-associated molecular pattern (PAMP)-triggered immunity, effector-triggered immunity, plant basal defense, salicylic acid-mediated plant immunity, and systemic acquired resistance. Despite extensive research over many years, the precise molecular and biochemical functions of EDS1 remained unclear. Our findings indicate that EDS1 acts as a transcriptional co-activator with acid activation domains and interacts with transcription factors to activate the expression of plant defense genes.

简介：傅正擎，博士，美国南卡罗来纳大学生物学系教授。南京农业大学和夏威夷大学植物病理学硕士，2008年获得美国内布拉斯加大学林肯分校博士学位，2008-2013年在美国杜克大学董欣年院士实验室从事博士后工作，2013年加入南卡罗莱纳大学生物学系。先后获得美国NSF资助3项，2019获得南卡大学Breakthrough Star荣誉称号。长期从事植物-病原微生物互作等方面的研究，在水杨酸介导的植物免疫信号转导等方向取得了丰硕的科研成果。在Nature、Cell Host & Microbe、Trends in Plant Science、Molecular Plant、Science Advances、Journal of Integrative Plant Biology、 Plant Physiology、New Phytologist、Journal of Experimental Botany等学术期刊发表多篇高水平学术论文。

学术讲座：中央财经大学张学勇教授—P2P爆雷对投资者风险偏好的影响

  （钟沛基 2022280095）

讲座题目：P2P 爆雷对投资者风险偏好的影响

讲座时间：2022年5月20日（周五）16:00-17：30

讲座地点：腾讯会议（会议号：988-502-961）

主讲嘉宾：张学勇教授  中央财经大学

主讲嘉宾简介：

　　张学勇，现任中央财经大学研究生工作部部长，研究生院院长，龙马学者特聘教授，金融学院教授、博士生导师，国家社会科学基金重大项目主持人，黄大年教学团队主要成员，北京市课程思政名师，中国资产管理研究中心主任。

　　2007年于浙江大学获经济学博士，2007-2009年在清华大学经济管理学院金融系从事博士后研究。主持国家社会科学基金重大项目1项，国家自然科学基金2项，北京市课程思政示范课1门，中国博士后基金1项，并在Journal of Corporate Finance，Financial Management，Journal of Economic Dynamics and Control，Journal of Portfolio Management, Journal of Accounting and Public Policy,《经济研究》、《经济学（季刊）》、《管理科学学报》、《金融研究》、《中国工业经济》等重要期刊发表论文多篇。

讲座主要内容：

Using staggered difference-in-differences (Staggered DID) methodology, we find that after the P2P collapse, local stock investors become more risk-averse, which is manifested in a significant reduction in their stock market trading activity. To further confirm that investors become less likely to take risk, we find that the local collapse makes investors less inclined to buy stocks of high-volatility and high-beta. Investors in affected areas are also less willing to transfer funds to the stock market, and less likely to trade unfamiliar stocks. In addition, to ruled out other possible channels, we find that investors' trading performance does not change significantly after the collapse, excluding the cognitive impairment channel. There is no significant difference between investors' trading behaviors on stocks with good and bad firm fundamentals, indicating that the results are not caused by rational Adjustments.

【学术讲座】Antenna Technologies and Challenges for “Human x Car x Home” smart ecosystem
电子与信息工程学院　2024/5/20 14:29:00
  （钟沛基 2022280095）

报告人：魏鲲鹏 小米集团手机部首席天线专家  

邀请人：袁涛 教授

时间：2024年6月3日10:00-11:00

地点：深圳大学沧海校区致信楼N710



报告摘要：

The "Human x Car x Home" smart ecosystem is a rapidly growing field that aims to seamlessly integrate technology into our daily lives. Antenna technologies play a crucial role in this ecosystem, enabling communication between devices and providing connectivity to the internet. However, there are several challenges that must be addressed in order to fully realize the potential of this ecosystem, such as satellite direct to cell, 5.5G Communication, Digital wireless car key. These challenges include the need for antennas that are small, low-cost, and capable of operating in a wide range of frequencies. Additionally, antennas must be able to operate in complex environments, such as inside buildings and vehicles, where signal interference and multipath propagation can be significant. This report will explore the current state of antenna technologies for the "Human x Car x Home" smart ecosystem, as well as the challenges that must be overcome to enable widespread adoption of these technologies.

 

报告人简介：

魏鲲鹏博士，毕业于清华大学电子工程系，现任小米集团手机部首席天线专家，天线技术预研负责人，IET Fellow，中国信通院特级外聘专家，深圳大学校外博导。目前研究的重点是人车家全生态的无线通信技术，包括终端MIMO多天线技术，终端卫星通信技术，5.5G/毫米波技术，UWB无线技术及探索电磁波与人体之间的相互影响。现担任IEEE Open Journal of Antennas and Propagation副主编，IET Electronics Letter副主编，中国通信协会天线与射频分委会委员，已发表论文50余篇，包括IEEE TAP、IEEE AWPL等本领域高水平期刊，参编著作1部，获得授权发明专利60余项，其中美国专利20余项。拥有10多年消费电子无线通信研发经验，先后在华为、荣耀、小米等公司担任高级技术专家或总监职务，曾提出meta-loop、meta-line、多天线多频解耦等终端天线技术，在5G智能手机、平板/PC和家庭路由器等几十余款产品中商用。

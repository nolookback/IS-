讲座名称：Rich task representations in the human brain

讲座时间：2024年11月27日上午10：00

讲座地点：深圳大学致理楼L3-1336

主讲人：江界峰


讲座简介：

A hallmark of human intelligence is goal-directed behavior. In other words, for the same sensory input, our brain can process it flexibly and generate different responses that suit different goals. This flexibility is supported by a set of cognitive functions termed cognitive control. While much progress has been made towards the understanding of how cognitive control guides other cognitive functions to achieve goal-directed behavior, the origins of the control signals are understudied. Recent advances in behavioral paradigms and neuroimaging methods allow us to investigate this question through the lens of task representations, which encode key information about the goal, input, output and their transformations. Here, I will present two studies to show that task representations are rich, such that they may contain other information that signals the demand of cognitive control and benefits task performance.


主讲人简介：

江界峰，杜克大学 (Duke University) 心理学博士，现任美国爱荷华大学 (University of Iowa) 心理与脑科学系助理教授，曾先后在浙江大学，中国科学院自动化研究所，杜克大学及斯坦福大学 (Stanford University) 学习工作。研究工作主要使用认知心理实验，计算建模和神经影像技术研究认知控制及其与记忆和学习的交互。主持美国国家健康研究所 (National Institute of Health) 研究基金 (R01， 2022至今) 一项以及博士后研究基金 (F32，2017-2019) 一项。以第一/通讯/资深作者身份在高水平学术期刊发表文章17篇，包括Nature communications，Journal of Experimental Psychology: General，eLife ，Journal of Neuroscience，Neuroscience and Biobehavioral Reviews， Cerebral Cortex等。 长期担任美国自然科学基金(National Science Foundation)审稿人。

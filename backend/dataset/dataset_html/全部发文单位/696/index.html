【学术讲座】Defect Detection Using AI Approach for Urban Road Covers

摘要：

In urban areas, road covers are commonly used to secure large drains, chambers, and box culverts, which require regular inspections and maintenance. These covers are typically located on main roads with heavy traffic. However, conducting inspections by opening these road covers is both costly and causes traffic disruptions. Additionally, the existing inspection methods are limited due to variations in piping/channels, configurations, and unknown distances from entry points.

Previous studies have demonstrated that measuring vibrational signals can effectively detect structural faults from a distance. By harnessing the latest advancements in artificial intelligence (AI) technology, it is possible to analyze these signals. The objective of this project is to conduct a feasibility study on developing an AI algorithm for inspecting the structural health of road covers on main roads.

The proposed system will utilize state-of-the-art AI technologies to identify problematic signals originating from road covers amidst the background noise caused by environmental factors and traffic. Through the implementation of AI technologies, the system will be capable of distinguishing problematic road covers from the complex interferences of environmental and traffic noises. The outcome of this research will be a non-invasive inspection method that enables the differentiation between normal and abnormal road covers. This approach provides a cost-effective solution that minimizes traffic disruptions by eliminating the need to physically open the covers for inspection.

讲座内容简介：
The field of AI-driven intelligent
autonomous robotics is rapidly evolving
by blending sophisticated control strategies with cutting-edge machine learning techniques to push the boundaries of what robotic systems can achieve forward. This lecture delves into the journey of AI and robotics research from its inception to the present day, highlighting key developments and methodologies that have shaped the landscape of autonomous robotics. By highlighting key technologies, industry applications, and ground-breaking projects in intelligent control and AI-driven robotics, we demonstrate our tangible and impactful works including physics driven adaptive neural network control,  Barrier Lyapunov Function based control, optimized backstepping, and safe BLF-based reinforcement learning, and illustrate the depth of research and efforts required to advance autonomous robotics.
We
aim to work together with the forever young and energetic researchers to pursue excellence and innovation in this dynamic and impactful field of a future where AI and robotics seamlessly fused to address complex challenges for better humanity.

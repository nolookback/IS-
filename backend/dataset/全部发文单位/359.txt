讲座摘要：
In this talk, the recent advances in the organic and metal-organic molecules functionalized with various ligand moieties for biological and sensing applications will be discussed from the point of view of their structure–property relationship. In particular, our efforts to develop soft salt complexes and near-infrared phosphorescent metal complexes will be highlighted. Due to their great potential for applications in photodynamic therapy, signal processing, night-vision devices and information-secured displays, soft salts and near-infrared (NIR) light-emitting molecules have aroused growing interest recently. By considering the medical and optical applications, development of new materials is urgently needed. New and efficient strategies to develop soft salt complexes and NIR metallophosphors will be presented and their applications will be described.

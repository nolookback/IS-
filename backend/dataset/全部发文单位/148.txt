报告人简介：
Clotilde Policar is a professor in bio-inorganic chemistry at the Ecole Normale Supérieure (ENS-PSL) in Paris. After completing a Ph.D. focused on mimics of a metalloprotein at the Université de Paris-Saclay and Paris 5 universities, she was a post-doctoral fellow in the CEA working on high-field electron paramagnetic resonance spectroscopy to characterize complexes as spectroscopic surrogates of the Mn cluster from the oxygen evolving center in the photosystem II. She was then appointed an assistant professor at Université de Paris-Saclay, where she developed Mn-complexes that mimic the anti-oxidant protein manganese superoxide-dismutase (SOD).
